Video Stopwatch

Help: Using the Video Stopwatch

To use the stopwatch, you must first either record or import a video. Complete this step first.

Once you've uploaded your video, you'll notice two phrases that appear often, "start frame" and "end frame." The "Start Frame" is the exact time when the action you're recording starts. Right when the action you're recording occurs, pause the video, and click "Set Start Frame." The "End Frame" allows you to choose what time your stopwatch ends. Right when the action you're recording is completed, click "Set End Frame."

The "Segment Time" is how long the section you've chosen lasts. "Segment Time" is probably what you want to write down.
